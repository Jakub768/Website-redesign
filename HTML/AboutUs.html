Who are we?

BonziBuddy is a freeware desktop virtual assistant created by Joe and Jay Bonzi. Upon a user's choice, it would share jokes and facts, manage downloads, sing songs, and talk, among other functions. It can basically help you guide through the joys of internet!

What is the technology used behind Bonzi Buddy?

We are using Microsoft Agent to deploy your favourite purple Gorrilla.
